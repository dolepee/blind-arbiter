# BlindArbiter Demo Script

## Target Length
- 90 to 120 seconds

## Sequence

1. Open `https://blind-arbiter.vercel.app`
   Say:
   "BlindArbiter settles private milestone work without exposing the deliverable. Buyers escrow against a rubric, sellers submit sealed evidence, and the agent emits a redacted verdict that releases or disputes settlement."

2. Scroll the hero and proof summary
   Say:
   "The public site is read-only on purpose. It shows the proof trail, not the private artifact."

3. Show the Live Proof section
   Say:
   "This demo is backed by three real proof surfaces: Ethereum Sepolia escrow, Arkhai natural-language agreements, and Status receipts."

4. Open the Ethereum Sepolia escrow deployment and canonical lifecycle links
   Say:
   "This is the real escrow contract. The canonical case went through create, accept, submit, verdict, and release onchain."

5. Open the Status receipt links
   Say:
   "BlindArbiter does not expose the underlying work. Instead it anchors verdict and settlement receipts on Status."

6. Open the Arkhai links
   Say:
   "The same settlement logic was mirrored through a live Arkhai / Alkahest natural-language agreement lifecycle: create, fulfill, arbitrate, collect."

7. Return to the public site and show the canonical settlement card
   Say:
   "This card compresses the private workflow into what judges can verify: rubric, redacted verdict, recommended action, and the proof trail."

8. Close with the core claim
   Say:
   "BlindArbiter is a new escrow primitive for private deliverables. The work stays sealed, but the settlement is public and verifiable."
